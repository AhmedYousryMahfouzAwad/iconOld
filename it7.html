Granular management tools to secure, maintain and future-proof wireless infrastructures.

In today’s mobile-rich world, it’s vital that IT teams have the tools in place to observe network performance and to secure

the network from internal and external threats. Network visibility allows businesses to see how wireless is being utilized by

employees and visitors down to the application level. This can be used to implement wireless policies, restrictions and

security measures as well as improve engagement with customers by acquiring valuable data.







Services

Controller less management

Install and support controller less management solutions that are perfect for smaller networks. Allows moderators to

distribute network configurations across the entire network by integrating advanced controller functionality directly with the

access point.

Onsite managed wireless

For larger wireless networks, dedicated controller systems are more suitable. Dedicated onsite wireless controllers allow the

management of multi-vendor wireless networks such as Cisco, HPE and Juniper. This allows organizations to monitor client

behavior, manage capacity and troubleshoot application issues across large-scale wired and wireless networks

Cloud-managed wireless

Managing your wireless estate from the cloud is simple, effective and universally accessible from anywhere. Providing the

capability for IT teams to manage and configure their wireless estate from everywhere. Granular layer-7 visibility allows

administrators to see which user, devices and applications are being used across the network, allowing them to shape the

network’s policies accordingly.
