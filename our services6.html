Services

End-to-End Tests

Testing the flow, from start to end, of each, of the components that make up your software platform, is a fundamental

and crucial aspect of ensuring correct functionality.

Usability Testing

Usability testing is a technique used to evaluate a software product's Ul and UX by testing it on users. We record

usability metrics and provide actionable feedback you can use to improve your engagement.

Regression Testing

Regression testing can help you avoid falling into a bug-pit: a loathsome place where you're infinitely stuck taking a

step back for every step you take forward. Let our QA engineers help you plan a course forward, without any missteps.

Functional Testing

We craft black-box test cases based on the system requirement specifications (SRS) of your software platform, to help

you ensure functional compliance to those requirements.
